I am a star! Wanna know how my life cycle looks like?

In the vast cosmic narrative, a nebula is the opening scene—a swirling cloud of gas and dust, a cosmic storyteller's canvas, where the tale of a star's birth unfolds.

From this primordial stage emerges the protostar, a character cloaked in the promise of fiery transformation, yearning to ignite.

As it takes its place on the celestial stage, the protostar evolves into a main sequence star, the protagonist of our story, burning with the steady brilliance of youth.

Yet, time is unrelenting, and our star matures into a red giant, casting a nostalgic glow across the cosmic landscape.

But every star must face its final act, and the red giant meets its climax—a supernova explosion—a cosmic crescendo, scattering its elements across the universe, an epic finale to a celestial saga.
